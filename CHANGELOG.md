
# Pokémon Emerald2020 Changelog

This file contains the changes and additions that were made after incorporating DizzyEgg's pokemon and battle expansions. 

## Changes

### Money Lost After Whiting Out

When whiting out, money lost is calculated based on pokemon party level and number of badges collected.

*Money Lost = Number of Badges  * Highest Pokemon Level in the Party*

Badges| Base Penalty
--- | --- 
0 | 16
1 | 32
2 | 48
3 | 64
4 | 92
5 | 128
6 | 144
7 | 164
8 | 196

### Wild Double Battles
The number of wild double battles decreased, having a 1/3 chance to happening.
